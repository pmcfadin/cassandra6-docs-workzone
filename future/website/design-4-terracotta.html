Built for production

Why teams choose Cassandra

Not because it's easy. Because it's right for workloads that can't afford to fail, slow down, or be outgrown.

🌐

Truly Distributed

No master node means no single point of failure. Every node is equal, every write acknowledged across replicas. Your data is safe even when datacenters go dark.

🛡️

Fault Tolerant

Hardware fails. Networks partition. Cassandra keeps serving reads and writes through it all. Configurable replication means you choose your durability guarantee.

⚡

Consistently Fast

Sub-millisecond writes at any scale. LSM-tree storage means writes are always fast. Read paths are tunable. Latency stays flat as your cluster grows.

📈

Linearly Scalable

Adding a node adds proportional capacity — no resharding, no downtime, no surprises. Teams at Netflix, Apple, and Bloomberg scale by adding hardware, not rewriting apps.

🔄

Elastic by Nature

Nodes join and leave the ring seamlessly. Stream data rebalances automatically. Scale up for peak season, scale back when demand drops — without touching your application.

🔒

Enterprise Secure

TLS for all communications, role-based access control, audit logging, and transparent data encryption at rest. Compliance-ready out of the box.

Open governance, meritocratic community

Cassandra is an Apache Software Foundation project, governed by a Project Management Committee (PMC) elected by community merit — not corporate affiliation. Decisions are made in the open, on public mailing lists, by the people who do the work.
